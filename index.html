Why Us

We are Adamant about serving quality food and thus ensure to craft our menu by using freshest ingredients, secretly blended exotic spices from India, and profoundly proficient chefs collectively bring unique culinary delights every time with a dash of Care and a sprinkle of Canadian Love!

Our Story

As a child, Chef Ketan Patel was always selective about his meals packed in lunch boxes during school days to catering to friends at his birthday parties. Growing up and working in community kitchens and serving people during disaster relief works, Chef Ketan ensured to aid and learned the importance of wholesome and hygienic food to the needy. His love for quality and authentic food advanced him to opt for a career as a Chef. He studied Culinary, trained, and worked in Switzerland, England, and Canada, devoting rigorous hours behind the range to fine-tune his art while accepting the feedback and smiles from the guests delighted with his magic.

The Chef`s quest for uncompromising quality led him to build Taj Bistro, his visioned restaurant, in the year 2014. The restaurant`s brigade only focuses on delighting the guest with a never-ending trail of Indian Feast with exceptional Services.
